GoChat - Chat without saving contact (No ADS)

Utility App that allows to chat with anyone on WhatsApp without saving contact

GoChat is a great tool for your favourite messenger app to send messages to any numbers those are not saved in your contacts.

*Features*

-- Open Chat of Selected Number --

1. Select a 10 digit number anywhere and click on 3 dot menu.
2. Click on Open WhatsApp chat.

-- Send Selected Text through WhatsApp --

1. Select text anywhere and click on 3 dot menu.
2. Click on Share this through WhatsApp.
